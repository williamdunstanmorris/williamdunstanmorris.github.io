Kluster is a Max Package including midi-responsive OpenGL abstractions in Max/MSP that provide user-interfaces for easier OpenGL rendering.

## Abstract

This project explores whether the construction of visual-interactive systems can be accelerated for end-
user development. It focuses on the design of a real-time system that uses MIDI as a source of tracking improvisatory techniques in music to extend graphical composition capabilities for Max, a hybrid development environment for visual-programming. Congruous fields of research and development in the literature are researched and reviewed to inform the development of a series of software modules for Max. These modules are engineered to carry out specific operations pertinent to improvisatory performance and graphical composition. The findings reveal a more systemic approach to visual- interactive systems through design abstractions, and expose a series of future considerations related to the separation of improvisatory and graphical functionality.

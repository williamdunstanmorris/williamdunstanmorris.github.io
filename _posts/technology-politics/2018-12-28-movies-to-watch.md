## Thief (1981)

The perfect crime trope often crops up alongside another staple of the crime film: the ‘one last job’. For hardened criminals like James Caan’s Frank in Thief, that ‘one last job’ sums up the thief’s dilemma: he steals to get out of his life of crime, but the art of thievery is the only thing he knows. Frank takes on one last big jewellery heist when he starts to dream of a settled family life with cashier girl Jessie (Tuesday Weld).

I have been asked to be involved in an upcoming concert in San Francisco. The performance will involve a mixture of choreographed dancing, music and audiovisuals. Whilst concepts are still in discussion about what and how the concert will take place, I have noted some ideas in the post for how a tech rig could be setup, from a technological point of view. These are rig ideas that I have come up with in the past. If I had some more cash, I would definitely opt for Rig 3. See ya.

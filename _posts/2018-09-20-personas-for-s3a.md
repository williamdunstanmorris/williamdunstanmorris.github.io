Creating baseline personas of potential users for S3A software is a great way of discerning both the target audience and what the alpha tests will look like.

By satisfying these personas, we can start to create content on the website which will guide a specific user through a specific path of content/downloads/links. This technique is really great for generating content.

I’ll start by providing the personas here, and we can go from there:

Alpha-test 1:

Persona: A music engineer wants to try some new s3a plugins. He is creative but also technical, and is an avid DAW user. He has a knowledge of music hardware and software, and often combines the two throughout productions. He records in his bedroom, which comprises of a mixing desk, a laptop he uses for his mixes, a microphone and five speakers.

Alpha-test 2:

Persona: Has a BEng Electronic Engineering with Music Technology systems from the University of York. A good understanding of circuits, mathematics and beginner programming, which focused on Java Programming. In his final year, he specialised in Multimedia Sound Design, and was this is where he developed a strong passion for it. He has recently been inspired by a digital arts event, which has inspired him to look into doing an audio-based project. He is not sure what he wants to do though, and is still researching what technologies are available to him.

Alpha-test 3:

Persona: A researcher has heard about the VISR C++ framework at a conference he attended in Italy through word of mouth. He has a little experience in C++, and his research has primarily been around machine-learning in Python. He wants to download the VISR framework on this computer and set up a development environment for him to try some basic sound object panning across speakers using Python.

Alpha-test 4:

Persona: A member of the public attended one of the S3A events, and was particularly impressed with the Vostok K incident. She is interested in understanding what technology lies behind the Vostok K incident. She is particularly interested in the WIFI routing capabilities and wants to learn more about that.
